• On mobile:
    • Tap the word "CREDIT" to insert coins
    • Tap the playing field to start the game
    • Tap the left/right side to move your ship
    • Tap the center column to fire
    • You can also tap the credits bar to fire
  • On desktop:
    • C: Insert coins
    • S: Start game
    • Space: Fire
    • Left/Right arrows: Move ship
